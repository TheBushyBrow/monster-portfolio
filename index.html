About

I'm a web designer based out of Seattle, WA. I received my B.S. in Information Technology with a focus in web and database management from Central Washington University. I choose to work in web because... well, it's a weird, but crazy awesome mishmash of design and development. I enjoy the challenges of making a design work within the constraints of web development. I would say that my work is heavily influenced by Adventure Time, twizzlers, and doodles.
