Living Orange

Sticker Awards

When I started to work at ExactTarget, I joined a team that was just being put together with a bunch of new people. My manger and I thought it would be cool to recognize our teams efforts as we grows. So the idea of making badges or stickers came out. I was asked to come to up with something fun and collectable.

That's when I put this sticker together. I wanted to do something that was playful, bright and also capture our Orange spirit.

After many mocks, we settled on the Flying Hero which really fit what we thought our team members were. And now my team collects the stickers and compete to see who can get the most!
